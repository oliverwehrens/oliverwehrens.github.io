Conference talks 2024

May 24, 2024 - 1 min

I’m looking forward to pick up speaking at conferences again. It has been a long time since Covid.

Our Fuckups and What You Can Learn From It

I will talk together with André Neubauer this year at

  • Bedcon 2024 in Berlin Sep 19th - Sep 20th 2024
  • WJAX 2024 in Munich Nov 4th - Nov 8th 2024

We will share the most significant technical fuckups we’ve encountered across different companies during our career. We will offer a perspective on the causes, the impacts, and the lessons we learned.

By sharing our experiences, we aim to provide insights into the complexity and dynamics of failures in the tech world and how to mitigate them. It will be an entertaining and informative talk. Attendees will get practical tips on how to foster a culture of learning and resilience and embrace mistakes.

If you wonder how seemingly small decisions can have a big impact, this is your talk.

Come by and say hello.
