Service Discovery For Machines And Humans

Feb 1, 2017 - 1 min

My slides from the OOP 2017 conference.

  • Abstract:

Combining Continuous Deployment and a Microservice architecture brings new challenges to develop and operate your platform. A service discovery enables you to build a flexible system. Developers need to have an up to date view on the deployed services as well. A human readable registry with relevant information is needed. I will outline what we solved with a Service Registry and what impact it had on our architecture. Furthermore I will show what we needed to give our developers to get an up to date view on the whole platform.
